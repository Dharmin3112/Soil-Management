Laterite

Laterite is a soil type rich in iron and aluminium and is commonly considered to have formed in hot and wet tropical areas. Nearly all laterites are of rusty-red coloration, because of high iron oxide content.

Crop Informtion

Crop type Type of season Duration Crops Discription
Root Crops Kharif 6-24 months Cassava Best planted at the onset of the rainy season. Cassava can tolerate dry periods but performs better with consistent moisture.
Root Crops Kharif 4-6 months Sweet Potatoes Plant during the rainy season to ensure adequate soil moisture.Can be grown year-round with irrigation if necessary.
Legumes Kharif June To September Groundnuts (Peanuts) Plant at the beginning of the rainy season. Peanuts require well-drained soil and may suffer if excessive moisture is present.
Legumes Kharif 3-5 months Soybeans Best planted during the rainy season. Soybeans need consistent moisture and will benefit from the rain during their growing period.
Grains Kharif 3-4 months Maize (Corn) Plant at the start of the rainy season. Maize requires adequate moisture for optimal growth and yield.
Grains Kharif 2-3 months Millets Best planted at the beginning of the rainy season. Millets are relatively drought-tolerant and can grow well with variable rainfall.
